Welcome to Westchester, LA!

Featuring

  1. Los Angeles International Airport
  2. Randy's Donuts
  3. In-N-Out Burger
  4. Playa Del Rey Beach

The gently curving streets, tidy homes and well-manicured lawns of Westchester create a small-town feel just a mile or so from busy LAX. Its proximity to tech hubs in Venice, Playa Vista, Culver City and El Segundo make Westchester an appealing home-buying destination for technology workers. Take a stroll in Westchester Park or eat at In-n-out Burger while watching airplanes landing at LAX. Don't miss the airplane display outside, nor the aviation history displays on the walls.
